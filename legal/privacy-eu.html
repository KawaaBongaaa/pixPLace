Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with GDPR.

Effective Date: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how pixPLace Ai (“we,” “us,” or “our”) processes personal data of users located in the European Economic Area (EEA), in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679.

By using the Service, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

1 Data Controller

pixPLace Ai acts as the Data Controller for personal data processed in connection with the Service.

Contact details:
pixPLace Support

If required under Article 27 GDPR (non-EU entity), an EU representative must be designated.

2 Categories of Personal Data Collected

2.1 Telegram Account Data

When accessing via Telegram, we may collect:

  • Telegram user ID
  • Username
  • Profile photo (if publicly available)
  • Language settings

We do not access private Telegram conversations outside bot interaction.

2.2 Usage & Technical Data

Automatically collected:

  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Browser type
  • OS
  • Access timestamps
  • Log files
  • Error diagnostics

2.3 Prompts & Uploaded Content

We process:

  • Text prompts
  • Uploaded images
  • Generated outputs

This processing is strictly limited to providing AI-generated results. We do NOT use user content to train AI models.

2.4 Transaction Data

Payments are processed exclusively via Telegram Stars. We receive:

  • Transaction confirmation
  • Subscription status
  • Credit balance

We do not receive payment card details.

3 Legal Basis for Processing (Art. 6 GDPR)

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

(a) Contract Performance (Art. 6(1)(b))

Processing necessary to:

  • Provide AI generation services
  • Deliver digital credits
  • Manage subscriptions

(b) Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f))

Processing necessary for:

  • Fraud prevention
  • Abuse detection
  • Platform security
  • Enforcement of Terms

We balance these interests against user rights.

(c) Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c))

Where required for:

  • Tax compliance
  • Legal requests
  • Regulatory obligations

(d) Consent (Art. 6(1)(a))

Where applicable (e.g., optional analytics or cookies). Users may withdraw consent at any time.

4 AI Processing & Third-Party Processors

User prompts and uploaded data are transmitted to third-party AI providers for content generation via API. These providers act as independent processors or controllers depending on technical structure.

pixPLace Ai:

  • Does not train AI models
  • Does not authorize training on user data
  • Implements appropriate contractual safeguards where applicable

5 International Data Transfers

Personal data may be transferred outside the EEA. Where transfers occur to countries without adequacy decisions, we implement safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), contractual protections, and security measures. By using the Service, you acknowledge such transfers.

6 Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary: for the duration of the user account, for legal obligations, for fraud prevention, and for dispute resolution. Upon account deletion request, data is removed unless retention is legally required.

7 Data Subject Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (Art. 15)
  • Rectify inaccurate data (Art. 16)
  • Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”) (Art. 17)
  • Restrict processing (Art. 18)
  • Data portability (Art. 20)
  • Object to processing (Art. 21)
  • Withdraw consent (Art. 7(3))

Requests may be submitted to: pixPLace Support. We may verify identity before responding. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

8 Automated Decision-Making

pixPLace Ai does not engage in automated decision-making producing legal or similarly significant effects under Article 22 GDPR. AI outputs are user-initiated and do not constitute profiling for legal decisions.

9 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including encrypted HTTPS communication, access controls, secure server infrastructure, and limited data access. However, no system guarantees absolute security.

10 Children’s Data

The Service is not intended for individuals under 16 years of age within the EU. We do not knowingly process data of minors.

11 Content Moderation & Safety

We may log prompts and usage data for abuse prevention, enforcement of Content Policy, and legal compliance. Such processing is based on legitimate interest and platform integrity.

12 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be indicated by updating the Effective Date. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of updates.
