High-Fidelity Studio

Nano Banana Pro

Our premium "Image-to-Image" engine. Pro is engineered for Subject Consistency—the ability to take a real person or object and place them in any scenario while keeping them 100% recognizable.

Quad-Reference (1-4 Photos)

Upload up to 4 photos. The AI calculates a 3D semantic map of the subject. Use this for "Digital Photoshoots" where you need the same person in different outfits or locations.

Smart Fidelity

Nano Banana Pro does not use a Strength slider. It uses Automated Subject Isolation—it knows which pixels belong to the face and keeps them sharp while rebuilding the rest of the world.

PRO TIP: For best results, upload photos with different angles (Front, Side, 3/4) to help the AI learn the full geometry of the subject.
Typography King

Flux Pro Advanced

The current State-of-the-Art in AI. Flux Pro excels at Human Anatomy (perfect hands/eyes) and Embedded Text. It is the only model that can reliably "write" on objects within the image.

Creative Control

Print Maker SDXL

Designed for Graphic Designers and Merch Creators. This is the only model in pixPLace that provides a manual Image Strength slider for granular control over transformation.

Strength 0.1 - 0.3

"Creative Exploration". The AI uses your image only for color palette and basic layout, generating 90% new content.

Strength 0.4 - 0.6

"Style Transfer". Best for taking a pencil sketch and turning it into a professional 3D render or vector illustration.

Strength 0.7 - 0.9

"Quality Restoration". Used to clean up low-res logos, remove artifacts, or add slight textures without changing the design.

Semantic Qwen Edit

The world's first Instruction-Based image editor. It doesn't just re-generate; it understands "verbs" and "objects".

Descriptive (Old Way)

"A man with a blue hat in a park"

Result: AI might change everything to fit the description.

Instructive (Qwen Way)

"Change his blue hat to a red crown"

Result: Everything stays same, only the hat changes.

Post-Processing Tools

Alpha Background Removal

Our segmentation AI is trained on studio photography. It identifies the "Subject" vs "Environment" and generates a clean PNG transparency. Ideal for creating stickers or website assets.

Pixel-Perfect Upscaling

Unlike simple bicubic scaling, our HD Upscaler "re-paints" missing details. It uses Generative Super-Resolution to sharpen edges and hallucinate fine textures (like skin pores or fabric weave) that weren't in the original.

Video Generation

Turn static frames into Motion Masterpieces.

Frame Continuity

We use the 1st frame as a strict anchor. The AI calculates "optical flow" to animate hair, water, and camera pans while preserving the original image's look.

Music & Sound (Suno)

Full-spectrum audio generation. From ambient soundscapes to 2-minute vocal tracks. Use our Style Chips to instantly set the BPM, mood, and genre.
